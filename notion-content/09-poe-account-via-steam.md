# 🎮 Creating a Path of Exile Account via Steam

> Source: <https://www.notion.so/2af7c5e794868054bc71fb1b69560f54>

This process allows you to play the game both through Steam and the standalone Path of Exile client.

## Phase 1: Create and Link the Account

1. **Access the Path of Exile Website and Select Steam Sign-in**
   - Go to the official Path of Exile website (`pathofexile.com`).
   - On the sign-in screen, select the **Steam** option.

2. **Sign in to Steam**
   - A Steam login window will appear. Enter your **Steam Account Name** and **Password** and click **Sign in**.

3. **Create a Path of Exile Account Name**
   - Since your Steam account is not yet associated with a PoE account, you will be prompted to create one.
   - Enter your desired **Account Name**.
   - Check the boxes to **I ACCEPT THE TERMS OF USE** and **I ACCEPT THE PRIVACY AND COOKIES NOTICE**.
   - Click the **CREATE ACCOUNT** button.

4. **Account Creation Success**
   - You will see a confirmation screen that your account has been successfully created and you are now logged in.

## Phase 2: Connect an Email Address and Set a Password

1. **Access Manage Account**
   - While logged in on the Path of Exile website, navigate to **My Account** then **MANAGE ACCOUNT**.
   - Look under the **Primary Login** section. It should currently show **Email (None)**.
   - Click the **CONNECT** button next to **Email (None)**.

2. **Enter Email Address**
   - You will then receive a confirmation email.

3. **Check Your Email and Confirm Link**
   - Check the inbox of the email address you provided. You should have received a confirmation email from Path of Exile.
   - Click the confirmation link within the email. This will take you back to the PoE website where you must click **CONFIRM LINK**.

4. **Create a Password**
   - You will be prompted to create a password for your new email login. This password will be used when logging in via the standalone Path of Exile client.
   - Enter and confirm your desired **PASSWORD**.
   - Click **SUBMIT**.

5. **Verification**
   - Go back to the **MANAGE ACCOUNT** page.
   - The **Primary Login** section should now display your linked email address with a masked portion.
   - Your Steam ID is now listed as a **Secondary Login**.

You can now log in to the Path of Exile website or the standalone game client using your linked **Email** and the **Password** you created.
