# How do you link your Steam account to your Path of Exile account

> Source: <https://www.notion.so/1dd7c5e7948680cdbaa8ec46ed846963>

*Cheapest top-up method*

To link your Steam account to your Path of Exile (PoE) account, follow these steps:

1. **Account Linking**
   - Visit the Path of Exile website.
   - Go to the "Manage Account" section.
   - <https://www.pathofexile.com/my-account/connections>

2. **Following the Prompts**
   - Follow the on-screen instructions to link your Steam account, if not already prompted.

3. **Confirm the connection using the link in your Path of Exile email.**

4. **Verify that all steps are completed successfully.**

## How to Accept a Steam Gift

### 1. Check Your Notifications

When someone sends you a gift, a **green notification bell** will appear in the top right corner of your Steam client or the Steam website.

- Click the **Bell Icon**.
- Select the notification that says **"1 New Gift"** or **"You received a gift!"**

### 2. View the Gift Pending Page & Accept the Gift

Once you click the notification, you will be taken to a gift invitation screen. Here you will see:

- The name of the game (e.g., *Path of Exile 2*).

### 3. Confirm to Library

After clicking "Accept Gift," a small confirmation pop-up will appear.

- Click **Add to my library**.
- The game is now permanently tied to your account!
