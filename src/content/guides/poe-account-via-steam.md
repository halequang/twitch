This process allows you to play the game both through Steam and the standalone Path of Exile client.

### Phase 1 · Create and link the account

1. **Access the Path of Exile website** (`pathofexile.com`) and select the **Steam** option on the sign-in screen.
2. **Sign in to Steam** — enter your Steam account name & password.
3. **Create a PoE account name** — enter your desired name, accept the Terms of Use and Privacy & Cookies Notice, then click **CREATE ACCOUNT**.
4. **Account creation success** — confirmation screen appears, you're logged in.

### Phase 2 · Connect email and set password

1. **Access Manage Account** — under **Primary Login** click **CONNECT** next to "Email (None)".
2. **Enter email address** — you'll receive a confirmation email.
3. **Check your email and confirm** — click the confirmation link, then **CONFIRM LINK** on the PoE site.
4. **Create a password** — used when logging in via the standalone PoE client. Click **SUBMIT**.
5. **Verification** — Primary Login now displays your email (masked); Steam ID is listed as Secondary Login.

You can now log in to the PoE website or standalone game client using your linked **email** and **password**.
