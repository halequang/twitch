---
title: How to Claim Your Twitch Drop Rewards
subtitle: Nhận thưởng Twitch Drop cho Path of Exile
icon: "🎁"
order: 1
---

1. **Login to the Path of Exile website.** Go to the Connection tab and click "Connect" under the Twitch link.
   <https://www.pathofexile.com/my-account/connections>
2. **Login to Twitch** and authorize the connection.
3. **Check your rewards.**
4. **Go in-game and select your skin.** On PC, press `K` to open cosmetics.
